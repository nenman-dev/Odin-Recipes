Jollof Rice Recipe

A Plate of Delicious Nigerian Jollof Rice

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon
  • 1 large onion,sliced
  • 2 (14.5 ounce) cans stewed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups of water

Steps

  1. Pour oil into large saucepan. Cook onion in oil over medium heat until transluent
  2. Stir in stewed tomatoes and tomato paste, and season with salt, black pepper, cayenne pepper,red pepper flakes and rosemary. Cover and bring to a boil. Reduce heat, stir in water and chicken pieces.Simmer for 30 minutes
  3. Stir in rice,carrits and green beans and season with nutmeg. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low. Cover and simmer until the chicken is fork tender and the rice is cooked for 25 to 30 minutes
